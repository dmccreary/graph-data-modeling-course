# Digital Twins

## Summary

This chapter explores how graphs enable digital twin implementations. We cover building models, manufacturing systems, supply chain twins, real-time updates, and the unique advantages graphs provide for digital twin applications.

## Concepts Covered

1. Digital Twins
2. Building Models
3. Manufacturing Models
4. Supply Chain Models
5. Real-time Updates
6. Graph Advantages DT

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, students will be able to:

- Define digital twins and their role in modern systems
- Model buildings, manufacturing, and supply chains as graphs
- Design real-time update mechanisms for twin synchronization
- Explain graph advantages for digital twin implementations
- Connect sensor data to digital twin models
