# Healthcare Modeling

## Summary

This chapter covers comprehensive healthcare data modeling in graphs. We explore patient and provider modeling, clinical data, claims processing, population health, benefit plans, FHIR standards, and clinical decision support systems.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, students will be able to:

- Design patient and provider nodes with clinical attributes
- Model claims and coverage relationships
- Build population health models tracking disease spread
- Implement FHIR-compliant data models
- Apply the four-level FHIR approach to clinical knowledge
- Create care path models for treatment optimization
- Design clinical decision support systems
