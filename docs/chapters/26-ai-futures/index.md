# AI and Graph Futures

## Summary

This final chapter explores the future of graph data modeling in the age of AI. We examine how graphs and AI will converge, the role of world models in intelligent systems, and emerging trends that will shape the field.

## Concepts Covered

1. Graph AI Future
2. World Models
3. AI and Graph Future

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, students will be able to:

- Predict trends in graph and AI integration
- Explain the role of world models in AI systems
- Evaluate emerging graph technologies
- Design forward-looking graph architectures
- Connect course concepts to future developments
- Articulate the value proposition of graph data modeling for AI
